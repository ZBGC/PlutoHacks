PlutoReach

PlutoReach is a robotic arm brace that aims to assist in regaining movement in the arm.




Who Will This Help?

NICU New Borns

A miniture version of this arm brace will be able to help you excersize your newborn's arm so they can have maximum functionality in the future.

Stroke Survivors

A larger version of this arm will be able to assist teens and adults who need to work on the range of their arm due to the result of a stroke

Kids

A version of this can be made for kids needing therapy due to a broken arm.
